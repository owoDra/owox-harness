# Task Statuses

- `intake`: request captured, intent still incomplete
- `intent_clarifying`: gathering missing intent and constraints
- `intent_confirmed`: intent is explicit enough to plan
- `planning`: task contract and checks are being prepared
- `executing`: implementation or document work is in progress
- `verifying`: evidence and required checks are being evaluated
- `awaiting_human_gate`: blocked on a required human decision
- `done`: verified and closed
- `blocked`: cannot continue without missing input, evidence, or permission
